About Moonu

Founded in 2024, MoonMate emerged from a personal journey of understanding the unique challenges that individuals with ADHD face in maintaining consistent sleep routines. Our team of neurodiversity advocates, sleep specialists, and technology experts came together with a shared vision: to create a solution that truly understands and supports the ADHD mind.

We believe that everyone deserves quality sleep, and our mission is to make bedtime routines more accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable for the ADHD community. Through continuous research, user feedback, and innovative technology, we're committed to evolving our platform to better serve our users' needs.
