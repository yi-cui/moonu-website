Sleep Challenges

Why sleep is harder with ADHD

Many people with ADHD face unique challenges when it comes to bedtime and sleep quality. Moonu addresses these specific pain points to help you rest better.

Racing Thoughts

When it's time to sleep, your mind suddenly becomes hyperactive, racing with thoughts, ideas, and worries that make it impossible to relax and drift off to sleep.

Delayed Sleep Phase

Your internal clock is offset from typical sleep patterns, making it difficult to fall asleep at a conventional bedtime and nearly impossible to wake up feeling refreshed.

Inconsistent Routines

Establishing and sticking to a bedtime routine feels nearly impossible. The struggle to maintain consistency means your body never develops healthy sleep cues.

Revenge Bedtime Procrastination

After a day of forcing focus, you finally feel free at night, leading you to delay sleep to enjoy that freedom—even though you know you'll regret it tomorrow.

Sound familiar?

Moonu is specifically designed to address these ADHD sleep challenges with features tailored to your unique needs.
